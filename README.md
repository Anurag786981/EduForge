# EduForge Platform

A cloud-based Coaching Institute Management Platform designed to simplify student management, attendance tracking, fee management, and question paper generation.

---

## Overview

EduForge helps coaching institutes digitize their day-to-day operations through a centralized web application.

The platform focuses on reducing manual work and improving operational efficiency for teachers and coaching administrators.

---

## Features

### Student Management

* Student Registration
* Student Search
* Batch Assignment
* Student Profiles

### Attendance Management

* Daily Attendance
* Monthly Attendance Reports
* Attendance Dashboard

### Fee Management

* Fee Collection Tracking
* Due Fee Monitoring
* Payment History

### Question Bank

* Subject-wise Questions
* Chapter-wise Categorization
* Difficulty Level Management

### Question Paper Generation

* Automated Question Selection
* Marks Distribution
* PDF Export

### Dashboard

* Total Students
* Attendance Summary
* Fee Collection Summary
* Upcoming Exams
