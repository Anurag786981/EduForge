EduForge Dashboard Mockup Recommendations

Three dashboard design directions for the MVP, each translated across Principal, Admin, Teacher, and Parent views. The layouts prioritize the role outcomes from the requirements PDF: operational overview, records and finance workflows, classroom execution, parent confidence, pickup confirmation, and visible Question Bank / Paper Generator entry points.

MVP modules covered

  • Role-based dashboards and role-specific navigation.
  • Attendance, fees, approvals, notifications, calendar, pickup, and maintenance.
  • Teacher workflows for timetable, attendance, progress, homework, feedback, and questions.
  • Parent-facing child progress, school activity media, fee state, homework, and pickup status.
